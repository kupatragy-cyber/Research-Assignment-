# Research-Assignment- Business Analytics Terminology

Research Assignment - Business Analytics Terminology
📊 Business Analytics Concepts & KPIs Assignment
📌 Overview

This project is an academic assignment focused on foundational concepts in Business Analytics, Data Analytics, and Marketing Performance Measurement. It provides clear definitions, real-world examples, and credible sources for key terms used in modern data-driven organizations.

The goal of this assignment is to demonstrate a strong understanding of how businesses use data to make decisions, measure performance, and optimize marketing efforts.

🎯 Objectives
Explain core analytics concepts in simple, original language
Demonstrate understanding through real-world business examples
Apply proper academic research and referencing
Show how analytics supports decision-making in organizations
📚 Contents

The assignment is divided into four main sections:

1. Foundational Concepts of Analytics

Covers the basics of analytics and decision-making, including:

Business Analytics
Data Analytics
Business Intelligence (BI)
Big Data
Descriptive, Diagnostic, Predictive, and Prescriptive Analytics
Data Literacy
2. Data, Measurement & Reporting

Focuses on how data is structured, stored, and used:

Structured vs Unstructured Data
Data Warehouse
Data Mining
Data Visualization
Dashboards
Metrics vs KPIs (key distinction)
Benchmarking
Segmentation
3. Customer & Sales KPIs

Examines key performance indicators used to measure business growth:

Conversion Rate
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Churn & Retention Rates
Net Promoter Score (NPS)
Average Order Value (AOV)
Sales Funnel

🔑 Key Insight:
The CLV:CAC ratio is highlighted as a critical metric for business sustainability.

4. Marketing & Campaign Analytics

Explores how marketing performance is tracked and optimized:

Marketing Campaigns
Post-Campaign Analysis
Incremental Revenue
ROI & ROAS
Click-Through Rate (CTR)
Bounce Rate
A/B Testing
Lead Generation
Marketing Attribution

📈 Key Insight:
This section emphasizes how campaign data flows from execution → measurement → optimization → evaluation.

🧠 Key Learnings
Data-driven decision-making improves business outcomes
Not all metrics are equally important — KPIs drive strategy
Marketing success depends on accurate measurement and attribution
Customer profitability is best understood through CLV vs CAC
Analytics connects past performance to future strategy
